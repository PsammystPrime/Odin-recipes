Desciption

This is a simple dish that requires wheat flour,warm water,one table spoon of sugar,a rolling stick,a rolling chair and an oven and oil

Ingredients

  • wheat flour
  • warm water
  • sugar
  • oil
  • rolling stick
  • rolling table

steps

  1. mix the flour with warmwater
  2. add sugar to the mixture
  3. cut the dough into smallerportions
  4. roll the portins using a rolling stick and table
  5. place oil on the oven
  6. place the rolled portions on the oven
